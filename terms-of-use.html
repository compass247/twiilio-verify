2. Description of Services

CMAS provides AI-powered medical administrative services to licensed healthcare providers, including:

  • S1 — Clinical Note Entry: AI-assisted transcription and generation of clinical notes (SOAP format) from audio, dictation, or text input
  • S2 — Clinical Coding & Charge Capture: Automated ICD-10 diagnosis coding, CPT procedure coding, and charge entry for billing purposes
  • S3 — Clinic Message Handling: AI-powered triage, prioritization, and draft responses for clinical messages including prescription refills, lab results, referrals, and patient inquiries
Important: Our Services are designed to assist healthcare providers — not replace clinical judgment. All AI-generated outputs require physician review and approval before becoming part of the medical record.
